WE PARTY LIKE IT’S 1975


Disco Tehran is an underground party and performance project in the Lower East Side, NYC. This dreamboat connects NYC to the era of 1970's cosmopolitan venues in Tehran. It is a funky warm discothéque with live shows by local and international bands.

Each party explores groovy household throwbacks to rare music from the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and Latin America.

Disco Tehran is the lush party you saw in the movies. The party you always wanted to find.

Time/Place is will be announced prior to each show.
