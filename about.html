WE PARTY LIKE IT’S 1975

Disco Tehran is an underground party and performance project in the Lower East Side, NYC. It is a tunnel from Manhattan to the 1970s venues of Tehran, the cosmopolitan dreamboat. It is a funky warm discothéque with live shows by local and international bands.

Each party explores groovy household throwbacks to rare music from the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and Latin America.

Disco Tehran is the lush party you saw in the movies. The party you always wanted to find.
