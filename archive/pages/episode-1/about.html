Disco Tehran is an underground party and performance project in the Lower East Side, NYC. It is a funky warm discothéque with live shows by local and international bands. Disco Tehran explores groovy household throwbacks to rare music from the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and Latin America.


This dreamboat connects NYC to the era of 1970s cosmopolitan venues in Tehran.
